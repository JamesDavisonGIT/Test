Arrangement notes

Tempo: default 70 BPM, adjustable from 58–84 BPM. Slightly faster than the attached version, but still spacious.

Melody: verses are lowered and narrowed; pre-choruses retain tension; the final chorus is allowed to rise more.

Timing: lines are allocated musical phrase lengths. Short lines are held, long lines are spread out. This should feel closer to a singer phrasing lyrics rather than a sequencer trying to fit text into a fixed box.

Instrumentals: after the first chorus the song breathes; after the second chorus a longer, drawn-out guitar-style solo with delay/distortion raises emotion before the self-control bridge.

Ending: final chorus goes cinematic with choir, staccato violin pulse, toms, and percussion; the god-control outro drops to piano and choir only, ending spiritually.
